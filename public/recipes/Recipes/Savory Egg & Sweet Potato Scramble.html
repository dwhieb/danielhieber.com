Savory Egg & Sweet Potato Scramble

Breakfast

Prep 35 min. Makes 4 Difficulty Easy Source Skillet Recipes

Ingredients

  • * 8 eggs
  • * 1/2 C milk
  • * 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • * 1/4 tsp salt
  • * 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • * 1 tbsp butter
  • * 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • * 2 stalks green onion
  • * 2 C baby spinach
  • * parsley

Directions

# Preparation

* peel, quarter, and thinly slice sweet potatoes

* slice green onion

# Directions

1. In a medium bowl whisk together eggs, milk, cumin, salt, and pepper. Set aside.

2. In a large skillet melt butter over medium heat.

3. Add sweet potatoes and green onion, and cook about 8 mins or until potatoes are tender and lightly browned, stirring occasionally.

4. Add spinach and cook, stirring, about 1 minute or until slightly wilted.

5. Pour egg mixture over potato mixture in skillet, and cook over medium heat without stirring until egg mixture begins to set around the edges.

6. Using a spatula, gently lift the egg mixture so that the uncooked part flows underneath.

7. Continue cooking 2-3 mins or until egg mixture is cooked through but still glossy and moist.

8. Remove from heat.

9. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
