Sweet & Sassy Meatballs

Entree

Makes 16 Difficulty Easy Source Anyone Can Cook

Ingredients

  • 1 16-oz. can jellied cranberry sauce
  • 1 18-oz. bottle barbecue sauce
  • 2 1-lb. packages frozen cooked meatballs (32 per lb.)
  • Spaghetti

Directions

# Preparation

1. Thaw meatballs

# Directions

1. Begin cooking spaghetti

2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.

3. Stir together cranberry sauce and barbecue sauce.

4. Cook until cranberry sauce is melted, stirring occasionally.

5. Add meatballs to sauce and cook uncovered about 10 minutes or until meatballs are heated through, stirring occasionally.

Notes

Meatballs need to be thawed in advance of this recipe
