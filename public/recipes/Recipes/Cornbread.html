Cornbread

★★★★★

Bread, Breakfast

Ingredients

  • - 1 C flour
  • - 3/4 C cornmeal
  • - 2 tbsp sugar
  • - 2.5 tsp baking powder
  • - 3/4 tsp salt
  • - butter (for greasing pan)
  • - 1/4 C butter
  • - 2 eggs
  • - 1 C milk
  • - honey or honey butter

Directions

# Preparation

- Preheat oven to 400 degrees

# Directions

1. Coat pan with melted butter, or spray with cooking spray.

2. Stir together flour, cornmeal, sugar, baking powder, and salt, then set aside.

3. Beat eggs in a bowl.

4. Melt the 1/4 C butter.

5. Add melted butter and milk to the eggs and mix.

6. Add wet ingredients to dry, then stir until moistened and pour into pan.

7. Bake 15-20 minutes.

8. Serve with honey or honey butter.

Notes

Can be cooked with either a 10" cast iron skillet, a 9.5" round baking pan, or an 8x8" square pan.
