Quick Mozzarella Rolls

★★★

Appetizer

Prep 5 mins Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 roll or large brick of mozzarella
  • 2 tomatoes
  • basil

Directions

1. Dice tomatoes.

2. If using a mozzarella brick, slice lengthwise into thin slices. Top each mozzarella slice with tomatoes and basil, then roll up and seal by squeezing edge of cheese together with your fingers.

3. If using a sheet of mozzarella, first roll out the sheet, then add tomatoes and basil, then roll the sheet back up. Cut the sheet into 1/2" slices.
