Zesty Turkey Skillet

Entree

Difficulty Medium

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 turkey breast tenderloins
  • 1 C salsa
  • 1/4 C raisins
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 C water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 C couscous
  • 1/4 C slivered / chopped toasted almonds
  • parsley

Directions

# Preparation

1. Chop almonds if necessary.

2. Halve turkey breasts horizontally.

3. In a medium bowl combine salsa, raisins, honey, cumin, and cinnamon.

# Directions

1. Heat olive oil over medium-high heat.

2. Add turkey and cook ~2 min. ea. side, until browned.

3. Pour salsa mixture over turkey, bring to boiling.

4. Simmer, covered, for 10-12 min. or until turkey is no longer pink. Meanwhile, go to Step 5.

5. In a medium saucepan bring water and salt to boiling, then stir in couscous and remove from heat.

6. Cover couscous and let stand for five minutes.

# Serving

Fluff couscous with fork. Serve turkey and salsa mixture on top of couscous, and sprinkle with almonds and parsley.
