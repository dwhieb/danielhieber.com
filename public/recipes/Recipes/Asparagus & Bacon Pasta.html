Asparagus & Bacon Pasta

Entree

Makes 2 Source https://fee.org/articles/cooking-as-a-busy-millennial-with-no-money/

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • 1 package bacon
  • 10 stalks asparagus
  • grated parmesan
  • lemon juice
  • olive oil
  • red pepper flakes
  • salt & pepper
  • garlic powder

Directions

1. Start cooking enough pasta for 2 meals.

2. Meanwhile, crisp-cook bacon and snap off bottom ends of asparagus.

3. Chop the asparagus stalks into bite-sized pieces.

4. Heat some olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat.

5. Add the asparagus to the skillet, toss in oil to coat, and cook until soft, stirring occasionally.

6. Mix asparagus, bacon, and pasta together.

7. Pour in just enough olive oil to coat the pasta.

8. Add a dozen squirts of lemon juice, a couple shakes of red pepper flakes, as much cheese as you want, and salt, pepper, and garlic to taste. Stir and serve.
