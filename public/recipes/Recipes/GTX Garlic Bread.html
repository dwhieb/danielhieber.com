GTX Garlic Bread

★★★★★

Appetizer, Bread

Makes 4

Ingredients

  • - 1 French bread baguette
  • - 1/4 C butter
  • - 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
  • - 2 tbsp parmesan cheese
  • - 1 tsp parsley

Directions

1. Preheat GTX

2. Slice baguette

3. Melt butter

4. Whisk ingredients together in a bowl.

5. Brush one side of 4 slices of French bread with the mixture.

6. Place 2 slices buttered side down in the GTX, and the other two slices stacked on top, buttered side up.

7. Cook for 4-6 minutes.

8. Repeat with remainder of baguette.
