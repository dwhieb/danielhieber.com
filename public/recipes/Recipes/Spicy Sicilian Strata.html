Spicy Sicilian Strata

★★★

Breakfast

Prep 35 min Cook 45 min Makes 6 Difficulty Easy Source Skillet Recipes

Ingredients

  • 5 C 1" cubes French bread
  • 1 package (3.5 oz.) sliced pepperoni (preferably the small ones used for toppings)
  • 1/2 10 oz. package frozen spinach
  • 1/4 C oil-pack dried tomatoes
  • 1 C shredded Italian cheese blend
  • 3 eggs
  • 1.5 C milk
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 dash cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 C grated parmesan cheese

Directions

# Preparation

1. Thaw spinach in advance.

2. Chop peppers if necessary.

3. Drain spinach.

4. Drain and chop dried tomatoes

# Directions - Part 1

1. Spread bread cubes in a 15"x10"x1" baking pan and bake for 10 minutes, stirring once.

2. Coat a large, oven-safe skillet with cooking spray.

3. Transfer half of the bread cubes to the prepared skillet.

4. Top with half of the pepperoni, all of the spinach, and all of the dried tomatoes.

5. Sprinkle with 1/2 C of the Italian cheese blend.

6. Repeat layers with the remaining bread cubes, pepperoni, and Italian cheese blend.

7. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, Italian seasoning, salt, and cayenne pepper.

8. Slowly pour egg mixture over layers in skillet, then press down lightly with a spatula.

9. Sprinkle with rest of parmesan cheese.

10. Cover and chill for 2-24 hours.

# Directions - Part 2

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

2. Bake skillet, uncovered, for 35-45 minutes until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.

3. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.

Notes

Spinach must be thawed in advance, and mixture must be chilled for 2-24 hours.
