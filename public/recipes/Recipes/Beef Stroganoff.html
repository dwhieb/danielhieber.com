Beef Stroganoff

Entree

Makes 2 Difficulty Easy Source https://fee.org/articles/cooking-as-a-busy-millennial-with-no-money/

Ingredients

  • * 1 lb. ground beef
  • * curly noodles
  • * 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • * 2-3 tbsp. worcestershire sauce
  • * salt & pepper

Directions

1. Start cooking noodles.

2. Meanwhile, cook beef on medium heat.

3. When pasta and beef are done cooking, mix all ingredients together and serve.
