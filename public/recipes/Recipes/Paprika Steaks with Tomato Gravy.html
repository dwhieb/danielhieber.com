Paprika Steaks with Tomato Gravy

★★★

Entree

Prep 30 mins Cook Danny Makes 4 Difficulty Medium Source Skillet Recipes

Ingredients

  • # Steaks
  • 1/4 flour
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 4 beef breakfast or skillet steaks (4 oz. ea.), about 1/2" thick (or 2 larger steaks)
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 slices colby jack cheese

  • # Gravy
  • 6 small (not medium) tomatoes
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 tsp ground sage
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Directions

# Preparation

1. Cut tomatoes into wedges and seed them.

2. Mince garlic.

3. Measure out ingredients.

# Directions

1. In a small bowl stir together flour, paprika, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp pepper, then place mixture in a shallow dish for dipping the steaks in.

2. In an extra large skillet, preheat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium-high heat. Reduce heat to medium.

3. Dip each steak in the flour mixture, and add to the pan. Keep the leftover flour mixture.

4. Cook steaks for 4-5 minutes on each side. Prepare tomato mixture while cooking (next step). In the last 2 minutes of cooking, top steaks with cheese. When done, remove steaks from skillet and keep warm. Do not remove skillet from heat.

5. While steak is cooking, place tomatoes in a food processor. Cover and process with pulses until tomatoes are coarsely chopped.

6. When steak is done, immediately add 2 tbsp olive oil to skillet and heat.

7. Add garlic and cook for 1 min.

8. Add tomatoes, sage, salt, pepper, and any remaining flour mixture and stir, scraping the bottom of the pan.

9. Bring to boiling.

10. Reduce heat and simmer uncovered for about 5 minutes or until gravy reaches desired consistency.

11. Top steaks with gravy and serve.
