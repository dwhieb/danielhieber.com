Potato Frittata

★★★

Breakfast

Prep 25 min Makes 6 Difficulty Easy Source Skillet Recipes

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb. Yukon gold or russet potatoes
  • 2 large carrots
  • 12 eggs
  • 4 stalks green onions
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1/2 C yellow cherry tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp parsley or cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic

Directions

# Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

2. Thinly slice potatoes

3. Thinly slice carrots

4. Beat eggs lightly

5. Chop green onions

6. Halve the cherry tomatoes

7. Mince garlic

# Directions

1. In a 10" oven-safe skillet, heat oil over medium heat.

2. Add potatoes and cook for 5 min.

3. Add carrots and cook about 5 min more until carrots are tender and lightly browned, turning occasionally.

4. In a medium bowl whisk together eggs, half of the green onions, the salt, and the pepper, then pour the egg mixture over the potatoes.

5. Bake about 18 minutes or until frittata appears dry on top.

6. Let stand on wire rack for 5 minutes, moving on to step 7.

7. Meanwhile, in a small bowl gently toss together the remaining green onions, the cherry tomatoes, the parsley/cilantro, and the garlic.

8. Using a narrow spatula, loosen the edges of the frittata from the skillet, then cover with a serving platter and invert the frittata to the platter.

9. Top the frittata with the green onion topping and serve.
