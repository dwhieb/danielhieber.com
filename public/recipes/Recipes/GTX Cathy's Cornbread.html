GTX Cathy's Cornbread

★★★

Bread

Makes 3

Ingredients

  • - 1/2 C flour
  • - 1/2 C cornmeal
  • - 2 tbsp sugar
  • - 2 tsp baking powder
  • - 1/4 tsp salt
  • - 1 egg
  • - 1/2 C milk
  • - 1 tbsp vegetable oil

Directions

1. Preheat GTX and spray / grease wells.

2. Combine dry ingredients in a small bowl.

3. Beat egg, milk, and oil together vigorously by hand.

4. Pour the egg mixture into the dry ingredients and mix until just moistened.

5. Pour batter into wells and cook for 7-8 minutes.
