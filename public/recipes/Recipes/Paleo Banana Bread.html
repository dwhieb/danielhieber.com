Paleo Banana Bread

★★

Bread, Breakfast

Makes 1 loaf Difficulty Easy Source Paleo Magazine

Ingredients

  • - 6 large eggs, room temperature
  • - 4 medium bananas
  • - 1 C unsweetened sunflower seed butter
  • - 1/2 C honey
  • - 6 tbsp coconut oil (or butter)
  • - 2 tsp lemon juice (fresh if possible)
  • - 2/3 C coconut flour
  • - 4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • - 2 tsp baking soda
  • - 2 tsp ginger
  • - 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg
  • - 1/4 tsp ground allspice
  • - 1/8 tsp sea salt

Directions

# Preparation

1. Allow bananas to ripen over several days.

2. Set eggs out until they reach room temperature.

3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

4. Grease loaf pan with coconut oil.

5. Melt coconut oil.

# Directions

1. In a blender or food processor fitted with a steel blade, combine the eggs, bananas, sunflower seed butter, honey, coconut oil, and lemon juice. Blend until creamy and smooth.

2. Add the remaining ingredients and blend until smooth.

3. Pour the batter into the loaf pan and place in over for 40-45 minutes until brown and an inserted toothpick comes out clean.

4. Cool completely before removing from the pan and slicing.
