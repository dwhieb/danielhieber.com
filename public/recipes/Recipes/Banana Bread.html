Banana Bread

★★★★★

Bread, Breakfast

Makes 16 Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 2 C flour
  • 1.5 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  • 2 eggs
  • 5 medium ripe bananas
  • 1 C sugar
  • 1/2 C melted butter
  • 1/2 C brown sugar (optional)
  • 1 C chocolate chips (optional)

Directions

# Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

2. Grease loaf pan with butter.

# Directions

1. In a large bowl, combine dry ingredients except for sugar, brown sugar, and chocolate chips. Make a well in the center.

2. In a medium bowl whisk eggs, then add bananas, eggs, sugar, and melted butter, and mix either with your hands or with a pastry blender.

3. Add wet ingredients to dry, and stir by hand until moistened. Batter will be lumpy.

4. (optional) Fold in chocolate chips if desired.

5. Spoon batter into pan.

6. (optional) Sprinkle top of loaf with brown sugar if desired.

7. Bake 55-60 min, until toothpick comes out clean.

8. Leave in the loaf pan to cool for 10 minutes.

9. Remove from pan and let cool completely (making slicing easier).
