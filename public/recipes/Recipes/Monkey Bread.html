Monkey Bread

★★★★★

Dessert

Prep 30 min Makes Lots Difficulty Easy Source Jean Hieber

Ingredients

  • 3 tubes buttermilk biscuits
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 C white sugar
  • 1/2 C brown sugar
  • 1 stick butter

Directions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees

2. Cut biscuits into fourths (sixths if they're large biscuits)

3. Mix white sugar and cinnamon together in a bowl.

4. Spray or grease bundt pan.

5. Roll each biscuit piece in cinnamon sugar and add to bundt pan. Make more cinnamon sugar as needed.

6. Mix brown sugar and butter in a pan and bring to boil for a few minutes, then pour over biscuit pieces.

7. Bake for 30 min.

8. Serve warm.
