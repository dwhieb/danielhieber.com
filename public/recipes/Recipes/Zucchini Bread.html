Zucchini Bread

★★★★★

Bread, Breakfast

Makes 16

Ingredients

  • - loaf pan
  • - 1.5 C flour
  • - 1 tsp cinnamon
  • - 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • - 1/2 tsp salt
  • - 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • - 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • - 1 egg
  • - 1 C sugar
  • - 2 zucchinis
  • - 1/4 C cooking oil
  • - butter or spray for greasing pan

Directions

# Preparation

- Preheat oven to 350 degrees

- Beat egg in a bowl

- Finely shred the zucchini

# Directions

1. Grease the bottom of a loaf pan and set aside.

2. Mix together flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder, and nutmeg.

3. In a medium bowl, combine egg, sugar, zucchini, and oil.

4. Add zucchini mixture all at once to the flour mixture and stir just until moistened. Batter should be lumpy.

5. Spoon batter into pan and bake for 50-55 minutes.

6. Cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pan.

7. Wrap and store overnight before slicing.
