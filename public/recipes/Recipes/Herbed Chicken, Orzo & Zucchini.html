Herbed Chicken, Orzo & Zucchini

Entree

Prep 20 mins Cook Danny Makes 4 Difficulty Easy Source Skillet Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 C dried orzo pasta
  • 4 thinly-cut chicken breasts
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 medium zucchini and/or yellow squash
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tsp dill
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper

Directions

# Preparation

1. Halve the zucchini / squash lengthwise and cut into bite-sized slices

# Directions

1. Cook orzo according to package directions, then drain, cover, and keep warm in the same pan.

2. While orzo is cooking, sprinkle chicken with basil.

3. In a large skillet, heat 1 tbsp olive oil over medium-high heat.

4. Reduce heat to medium and add chicken to skillet and cook about 12 mins or until no longer pink, turning once. Remove chicken from skillet.

5. Add zucchini / squash to skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, about 3 minutes or until crisp-tender.

6. Stir in the cooked orzo, remaining 2 tbsp olive oil, vinegar, dill, salt, and pepper.

7. Serve chicken over orzo mixture.
