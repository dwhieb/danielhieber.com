Greek Layer Dip

★★★★★

Appetizer

Ingredients

  • - 1 carton (6 oz.) play yogurt
  • - 1 cucumber
  • - 1 red onion
  • - 1 tbsp mint flakes
  • - 1 container (10 oz.) plain hummus
  • - 1 tomato
  • - 1/2 C crumbled feta cheese
  • - pita bread

Directions

# Preparations

- Chop cucumber finely

- Dice red onion

- Chop tomato

# Directions

1. In a small bowl stir together yogurt, 1/4 C cucumber, 1 tbsp red onion, and mint, then set aside.

2. Spread hummus on the bottom of a 10" quiche dish or 9" pie plate.

3. Spread yogurt mixture in a layer over the hummus.

4. Sprinkle with 1/2 C of the chopped tomato, 1/4 C cucumber, and feta cheese.

5. Serve with pita bread cut into eighths.
