Avocado-Feta Salsa

★★★★★

Appetizer

Makes 12 (1/4 C ea.) Difficulty Easy Source Anyone Can Cook

Ingredients

  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/4 C red onion
  • 1 tbsp parsley
  • 1 tbsp oregano
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 C coarsely crumbled feta cheese (4 oz.)
  • Pita bread OR Tortilla scoop chips

Directions

# Preparation

1. Chop tomatoes

2. Halve, seed, peel, and chop avocado

3. Finely chop red onion (up to 1/4 C)

4. Mince garlic

# Toasted Pita Bread Wedges

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

2. Cut pita bread in half along its length (making two circular slices), then cut into 1/8 wedges.

3. Spread wedges on baking sheet, and cook for 10 minutes.

4. Serve hot or cold.

# Directions

1. In a medium bowl stir together tomato, avocado, onion, parsley, oregano, oil, vinegar, and garlic. Stir gently to mix.

2. Gently stir in feta cheese.

3. Cover and chill for 2 hours or up to 6 hours.

4. Serve with toasted pita bread or tortilla scoop chips.

Notes

This recipe needs to chill for 2-6 hours in advance.
