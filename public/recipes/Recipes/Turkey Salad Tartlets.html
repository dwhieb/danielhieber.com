Turkey Salad Tartlets

★★★★

Appetizer

Makes 30

Ingredients

  • - 2 packages mini phyllo dough shells
  • - 8 grape tomatoes
  • - 1.25 C chopped cooked turkey breast
  • - 3 slices bacon
  • - several stalks of green onions
  • - 1 avocado
  • - 2 tbsp mayonnaise
  • - 2 tbsp sour cream
  • - 2 tsp lime juice
  • - 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • - salt and pepper

Directions

# Preparation

- Remove phyllo shells in advance of serving to let thaw

- Cook bacon and then chop into pieces

- Chop turkey breast, if needed

- Chop green onions

- Quarter grape tomatoes lengthwise

- Chop avocado (only when you're ready to serve)

# Directions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together turkey, bacon, and green onions.

2. In a separate small bowl, combine mayonnaise, sour cream, lime juice, mustard, salt and pepper.

3. Add mayonnaise mixture to turkey mixture, stirring to coat.

4. If preparing in advance, cover and chill for up to 24 hours. Make sure to remove phyllo shells in advance to let thaw.

5. Add avocado to turkey salad mixture just before serving.

6. Spoon turkey salad into phyllo shells, and top each with a tomato wedge.

Notes

It's possible this recipe is supposed to have lemon juice rather than lime juice.

This recipe can be prepared up to 24 hours in advance. If preparing in advance, don't take out phyllo shells to thaw until a few hours before you're ready to serve.
