Spanish Scrambled Eggs

Breakfast

Prep 35 min Makes 4 Difficulty Easy Source Skillet Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 dash chili powder (optional)
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 1 small zucchini
  • 1 tsp dried cilantro
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 4 eggs
  • shredded parmesan or cheddar cheese (optional)
  • tortillas (optional)

Directions

# Preparation

1. Chop onion

2. Mince garlic

3. Chop tomatoes

4. Halve zucchini lengthwise and slice thinly

# Directions

1. In a large skillet heat oil over medium heat.

2. Add onion, chili pepper, and garlic, and cook about 5 mins or until tender, stirring occasionally.

3. Add tomatoes, zucchini, cilantro, and salt, and cook about 5 mins more or until tomatoes release their liquid and zucchini is tender, stirring occasionally.

4. Add eggs to skillet and cook until desired consistency, stirring occasionally.

5. Remove from heat, sprinkle with parmesan or cheddar cheese if desired, and cilantro.

6. Serve with tortillas.
