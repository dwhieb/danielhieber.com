Hummus

★★★★

Appetizer

Makes 4

Ingredients

  • - 1 can chickpeas / garbanzo beans
  • - 1/4 C tahini
  • - 1/4 C lemon juice
  • - 1/4 C olive oil
  • - 1/2 tsp salt
  • - 1/4 tsp paprika
  • - 1 tbsp parsley
  • - 1/4 tsp powdered garlic

Directions

1. Combine chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, paprika, and garlic in a blender or food processor. Cover and blend or process until smooth. Scrape sides several times as necessary.

2. Spoon onto a serving platter and sprinkle with parsley. Stir in feta cheese if desired.
