GTX Pancake Biscuits

★★

Bread, Breakfast

Makes 1

Ingredients

  • - 1/2 C Jiffy baking mix
  • - 1/4 C milk

Directions

Mix ingredients in a bowl then pour the mixture into 1 well and cook for 7 minutes.

Notes

Perfect for bacon, egg, and cheese biscuits or other warm breakfast items. You can cook an omelet in the other well at the same time.
