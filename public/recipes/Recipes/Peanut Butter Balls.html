Peanut Butter Balls

★★★

Appetizer, Snack

Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • - 2 C crunchy peanut butter
  • - 2 ripe bananas
  • - 2 tbsp whole flaxseeds

Directions

# Preparation

- Allow bananas to ripen for several days.

# Directions

1. Mix ingredients together with a fork and mold into balls.

2. Store separated by a layer of parchment.

3. Refrigerate 2 hours before serving.

Notes

Needs to be refrigerated 2 hours before serving.
