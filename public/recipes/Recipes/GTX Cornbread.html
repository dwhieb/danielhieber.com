GTX Cornbread

★★★★★

Bread, Breakfast

Prep 25 min Makes 4 Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 package Jiffy cornbread mix
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 C milk
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • honey (optional)

Directions

# Directions

1. Preheat GTX cooker with cooking pan inside.

2. Combine cornbread mix, cinnamon, egg, and milk.

3. Melt butter in microwave, then add to mix.

4. Stir mixture by hand. Batter will be lumpy.

5. Spray GTX pan, or coat with butter.

6. Pour 3/4 C of the mixture into each well, and cook for 10 min or until toothpick comes out clean. Repeat with the remaining batter, spraying the pan again between batches.

7. Serve with honey and butter, or wrap in foil to keep warm.
