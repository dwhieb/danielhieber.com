Wicked Good Chocolate Cake

★★★★★

Dessert

Makes 12 Difficulty Medium

Ingredients

  • # Cake
  • Cooking spray
  • Flour
  • 1 package Devil's Food cake mix (18.25 oz.)
  • 1 package chocolate instant pudding mix (4 oz.)
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 C sour cream
  • 1/2 C warm water
  • 1/2 C vegetable oil
  • 1.5 C semisweet chocolate chips

  • # Martha's Chocolate Icing
  • 1 C sugar
  • 5 tbsp butter
  • 1/3 C whole milk
  • 1 C semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

# Preparation

- Preheat oven to 350 degrees

- Lightly mist and flour bundt pan

# Directions

1. Combine cake mix, pudding mix, eggs, sour cream, warm water, and oil in a large mixing bowl (first mixing dry and wet ingredients individually, then adding wet ingredients to dry).

2. Blend on low for 1 minute.

3. Stop and scrape down sides.

4. Increase to medium and blend for 2-3 minutes more, scraping sides again if needed. Batter will be thick and well-combined.

5. Fold in chocolate chips a handful at a time.

6. Pour batter into bundt pan, smoothing out the surface.

7. Cook for approximately 45-50 min, or until the cake springs back when touched. Cake should have begun to pull away from the edge of the pan.

8. Remove from oven and run a long, thin knife around the edges, then invert immediately onto a cooling rack.

9. Immediately begin preparing Martha's Chocolate Icing (below).

10. When the icing is ready, spoon it over the top of the cake, covering all the exposed cake areas if possible.

# Martha's Chocolate Icing

1. Place butter, sugar, and milk into a medium-sized saucepan over medium-high heat.

2. Stir until the mixture comes to a boil (3-4 min).

3. Still stirring, let the mixture boil until the sugar dissolves (approx. 1 min)

4. Remove the pan from heat and immediately stir in chocolate chips.

5. Continue stirring until the mixture is smooth and the chocolate has melted.

6. Spoon the icing over the cake.

Notes

This recipe requires a bundt pan.
