Dutch Apple Pie

★★★★★

Dessert

Prep 20 min Difficulty Medium

Ingredients

  • 4 apples (Fuji)
  • 1 C sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 pie shell (9") unbaked
  • 1/2 C oats
  • 2/3 C butter
  • 1.5 C brown sugar, packed
  • 1.5 C flour
  • 1.2 tsp baking soda

Directions

# Preparation

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

2. Peel and slice apples (slices should be 1/4 circumference)

3. Measure out ingredients

# Directions

1. Combine apples, sugar, cinnamon, 2 tbsp flour, and salt in a large bowl and mix well. Place mixture in pie shell.

2. In a medium bowl, combine oats, brown sugar, 1.5 C flour, and baking soda. Melt better and stir in, until mixture is crumbly. Sprinkle over pie.

3. Bake 45-60 minutes, or until apples are tender. Don't allow the crumble topping to become too crispy.

Notes

Get the largest pie plate / crust possible. 8" is a little too small for this recipe.
