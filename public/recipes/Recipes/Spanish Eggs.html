Spanish Eggs

★★★★★

Breakfast

Prep 35 minutes Makes 4 Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • - large skillet with cover
  • - 1 tbsp olive oil
  • - 1/2 C chopped onion
  • - 1/2 C chopped green pepper
  • - 1 clove garlic
  • - 3 large tomatoes
  • - 1 small zucchini
  • - 1 tsp dried cilantro
  • - 1/2 tsp salt
  • - 4 eggs
  • - Crumbled feta cheese

Directions

# Preparation

- Chop onion, green pepper, and tomatoes.

- Halve zucchini lengthwise and slice thinly.

- Mince garlic.

# Directions

1. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat.

2. Add onion, green pepper, and garlic, then cook about 5 minutes until tender, stirring occasionally.

3. Add tomatoes, zucchini, cilantro, and salt, then stir and cook for 5 minutes more, stirring occasionally, until tomatoes release their liquid and zucchini is tender.

4. Break each egg into a cup, and then slip the egg into the tomato mixture, arranging them evenly.

5. Simmer, covered, for 5 minutes until whites are completely set and yolks begin to thicken.

6. Turn off heat and sprinkle with feta cheese and cilantro, then serve.
