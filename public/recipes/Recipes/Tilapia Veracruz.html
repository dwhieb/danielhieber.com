Tilapia Veracruz

★★★★

Entree

Prep 25 min Makes 4 Difficulty Easy Source Skillet Recipes

Ingredients

  • 4 tilapia filets
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 C sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 C rice (1 bag of instant rice)

Directions

# Preparation

1. Thaw fish in advance

2. Cut onion in small wedges

3. Mince garlic

4. Slice mushrooms, if not already

# Directions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet that has a lid, over medium heat.

2. Meanwhile, rinse fish, pat dry, and set aside.

3. Add onion and garlic to skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes until tender, stirring occasionally.

4. Meanwhile, start cooking rice.

5. Stir in tomatoes, mushrooms, oregano, salt, and pepper, and bring to boiling.

6. Add fish to skillet, and spoon tomato mixture over them.

7. Cover skillet, reduce heat, and simmer, for 8-10 minutes or until fish begins to flake when tested with a fork.

8. Serve with rice and/or bread..
