Potato Soup

Soup

Source https://fee.org/articles/cooking-as-a-busy-millennial-with-no-money/

Ingredients

  • * 3 C chicken broth
  • * 6 red potatoes, washed, with eyes removed
  • * 2 carrots
  • * peas (frozen or fresh)
  • * 2 cans of chicken breast
  • * salt & pepper
  • * rosemary
  • * thyme
  • * garlic powder
  • * onion powder

Directions

1. Pour chicken broth into a large pot and heat to boiling.

2. While broth is heating, cut potatoes into bite-sized pieces.

3. When broth is boiling, add the potatoes.

4. Chop up the carrots and add them to the pot after the potatoes have cooked for 5 minutes.

5. After another five minutes, add the chicken and peas.

6. Stir, breaking up chicken.

7. Cook until potatoes are soft.

8. Add salt, pepper, rosemary, thyme, and garlic to taste. Stir.
