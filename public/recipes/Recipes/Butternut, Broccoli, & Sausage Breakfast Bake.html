Butternut, Broccoli, & Sausage Breakfast Bake

★★★★★

Breakfast

Prep 1 hour Makes 4-6 Difficulty Easy Source Paleo Magazine

Ingredients

  • - 1 small butternut squash
  • - Coconut oil
  • - sea salt
  • - black pepper
  • - 1/2 lb. pork sausage (or turkey sausage)
  • - 1 C fresh broccoli florets
  • - 1/2 C chopped yellow onion
  • - 10 large eggs
  • - 2 tbsp coconut milk (or unsweetened almond milk, or whole milk)

Directions

# Preparation

- Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

- Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.

- Peel, seed, and chop butternut squash into 1/2" chunks.

- Chop broccoli into 1/2" chunks.

- Chop onion.

- Chop or crumble sausage.

# Directions

1. Add butternut squash to medium bowl. Melt 1bsp of coconut oil and toss with butternut squash.

2. Arrange squash on baking sheet and start them roasting for 25-30 min, until they are tender and brown in spots.

3. Grease 8x8 or larger casserole dish with coconut oil and set aside.

4. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook sausage for 3 minutes.

5. Add broccoli and onion, and cook, stirring occasionally, until the sausage is cooked through and the vegetables are tender (about 5 min.).

6. Remove from heat and set aside.

7. When the squash is done cooking, arrange it on the bottom of the baking dish, and then spread the sausage mixture over it evenly.

8. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, coconut milk, and a generous pinch of salt and pepper.

9. Pour the egg mixture over the sausage and vegetables.

10. Bake for 35-40 minutes until puffed and cooked through.

Notes

Recipe calls for an 8x8 pan, but the size of the onions, squash, and package of sausage tend to make this too small. Upgrade to a larger casserole dish to use the entire (small) butternut squash, half of a large onion, and 1 package of breakfast sausage.
