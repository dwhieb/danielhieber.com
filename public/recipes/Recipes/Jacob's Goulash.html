Jacob's Goulash

★★★★★

Uncategorized

Cook Jacob Hill Makes 4 Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. lean ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 small bag shredded cheese
  • pasta choice
  • paprika
  • salt and pepper

Directions

# Preparation

1. Dice onion

2. Dice green pepper

# Directions

1. Begin cooking pasta.

2. Combine all ingredients except cheese and tomato sauce in a large skillet and fry until meat is done and veggies are tender.

3. Add tomato sauce and heat through.

4. Remove sauce mixture from heat and melt in cheese.

5. Add sauce mixture to pasta and serve.

Notes

Consider adding cumin next time too.
