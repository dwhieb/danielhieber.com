Breaded Pork

Entree

Prep 20 mins Makes 4 Difficulty Easy Source Skillet Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 lb. boneless center-cut pork loin roast
  • 2 C corn bread stuffing mix
  • 1 egg
  • Olive oil

Directions

# Preparation

1. Crush the corn bread stuffing mix by placing it in a plastic bag and rolling it with a rolling pin.

2. Trim fat from meat.

# Directions

1. Cut meat into four slices.

2. Place each slice of meat between two pieces of plastic wrap.

3. Using the flat side of a meat mallet, pound meat lightly until about 1/4" thick.

4. Remove plastic wrap.

5. Place stuff mix into shallow dish.

6. Crack the egg into another shallow dish.

7. Dip each piece of meat first into the egg, and then into the stuffing mix, coating each side.

8. In a 12" skillet heat 1 tbsp of the olive oil over medium-high heat.

9. Add two of the meat slices, cooking for 2-3 minutes each side until the coating is golden brown and the meat is slightly pink in the center.

10. Remove meat from skillet, keeping warm, and repeat with the remaining two slices of meat.
