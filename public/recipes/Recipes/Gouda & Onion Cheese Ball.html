Gouda & Onion Cheese Ball

★★★★★

Appetizer

Difficulty Easy

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. cream cheese (whipped)
  • 1 C shredded gouda
  • 1/4 C butter
  • 1 tbsp whole milk
  • 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp green onion
  • 2 tbsp dried dillweed
  • 1/2 C chopped almonds
  • Crackers (for serving)

Directions

# Advance Preparation

- Needs to sit 4 to 24 hours before serving

- Set butter out to soften (not melt)

# Preparation

- Shred gouda, if needed

- Thinly slice green onions

- Chop almonds, if needed

# Directions

1. Add cream cheese, gouda, and butter in a bowl and let sit 30 min at room temperature.

2. Add milk and Worcestershire sauce and blend on medium until light and fluffy.

3. Stir in green onion and dill.

4. Cover and chill for 4 to 24 hours.

5. Shape mixture into a ball, and then roll it in the chopped almonds.

6. Serve cold, with crackers.

Notes

This recipe needs to sit 4-24 hours before serving.
