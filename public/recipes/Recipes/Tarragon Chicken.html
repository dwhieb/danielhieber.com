Tarragon Chicken

Entree

Makes 2 Source https://fee.org/articles/cooking-as-a-busy-millennial-with-no-money/

Ingredients

  • * 2 chicken breasts, cut in half
  • * curly noodles
  • * 1 small carton heavy whipping cream
  • * 2 carrots
  • * olive oil
  • * lemon juice
  • * tarragon
  • * salt & pepper
  • * mushrooms
  • * white wine

Directions

# Preparation

- cut chicken breasts in half

# Steps

1. Heat some olive oil in a large skillet (which has a cover) on high heat.

2. Add chicken breasts. Cook uncovered until bottom is golden brown, then flip and repeat.

3. As soon as both sides are browned, pour in whipping cream, and add enough noodles for 2 meals.

4. Add half a glass of white wine. Drink the rest.

5. Reduce heat to medium-high and cook, stirring once.

6. While waiting, cut up carrots and mushrooms.

7. When noodles are half done, add carrots and mushrooms.

8. Cook until noodles are tender and chicken is done in the center. The cream should be mostly absorbed by the noodles at this point.

9. Reduce heat to low and add a few squirts of lemon juice directly to the chicken.

10. Add salt, pepper, and tarragon to taste and stir.
