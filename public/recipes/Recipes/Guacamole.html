Guacamole

★

Appetizer

Makes 16

Ingredients

  • - 2 ripe avocados
  • - 1 onion
  • - 1 tbsp cilantro or parsley
  • - 1 tbsp lime juice
  • - 1/4 tsp salt
  • - 1 clove garlic

Directions

# Preparation

- Allow avocados to ripen for a few days

- Halve, seed, peel, and chop avocados

- Chop onion

- Mince garlic

# Directions

1. Blend all ingredients together in a food processor until the mixture is smooth.

2. Serve immediately or cover and chill for up to 24 hours.
