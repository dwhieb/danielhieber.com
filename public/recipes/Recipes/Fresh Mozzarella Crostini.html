Fresh Mozzarella Crostini

★★★

Appetizer

Makes 30

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette
  • 1 brick/roll mozzarella cheese
  • Roma tomatoes
  • olive oil
  • basil
  • balsamic vinegar

Directions

# Preparation

- Preheat oven to 375 degrees

- Slice Roma tomatoes thinly

- Slice baguette

# Directions

1. Coat slices of baguette with olive oil.

2. Place slices in oven for 5-15 minutes until desired toastiness.

3. Place one slice tomato, then one slice mozzarella, on each slice of toast.

4. Sprinkle with basil and balsamic vinegar, then serve.
