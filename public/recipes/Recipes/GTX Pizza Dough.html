GTX Pizza Dough

★★

Bread

Makes 8

Ingredients

  • - 2 C lukewarm water
  • - 4 C flour
  • - 1 envelope yeast
  • - 1 tsp salt

Directions

1. In a large bowl, stir together warm water, yeast, and salt.

2. Add flour one cup at a time, mixing until combined and scraping the bottom of the bowl.

3. Cover and let stand for 2 hours.

4. Dough is now ready to use or store.

5. Roll dough relatively flat, and divide into sections (large enough to roll into a small ball about 2-3" in diameter).

6. If desired, place dough in ziplocs and freeze for up to 2 weeks.

7. To cook, flatten each ball into a 6" round, and place in GTX. Add toppings as desired.

Notes

Needs at least 2 hours for the dough to set, and almost another 2 hours to cook them all in the GTX.
