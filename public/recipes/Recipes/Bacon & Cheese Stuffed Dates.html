Bacon & Cheese Stuffed Dates

★★★★★

Appetizer

Makes 16 Difficulty Medium

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bacon (or pre-crumbled bacon bits)
  • 1/4 C green onion (3 stalks)
  • 1/2 C crumbled blue cheese
  • 3 oz. whipped cream cheese
  • 2 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Black pepper
  • 16 Medjool dates
  • 2 cloves garlic

Directions

# Preparation

1. Set out cream cheese to soften in advance (if needed)

2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees (if not preparing in advance)

3. Crisp-cook and crumble bacon (if needed)

4. Slice green onion thinly

5. Pit dates

6. Mince garlic

# Directions to Prepare in Advance

Follow all the directions except cooking in the oven, and store the dates in a sealed container in the refrigerator until it is time to cook them.

# Directions

1. In a medium bowl, stir together bacon, green onion, and garlic.

2. Add blue cheese, cream cheese, mustard, and pepper, then stir to combine.

3. Spoon cheese mixture into dates.

4. Bake uncovered for 5-8 minutes or until heated through.

5. Serve warm.

Notes

Cream cheese needs to be softened in advance of starting preparation.
