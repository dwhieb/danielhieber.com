Garlic, Pork, and Sweet Potato Hash

★★★★★

Entree

Prep 30 mins Makes 4 Source Skillet Recipes

Ingredients

  • 3 small sweet potatoes
  • 1.5 lb pork tenderloin
  • 2 tbsp reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • black pepper
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 8 cloves garlic
  • 2 green onions
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 2 tbsp water

Directions

# Preparation

1. Scrub and chop sweet potatoes.

2. Thinly slice garlic.

3. Slice green onions.

4. Trim fat from meat.

5. Measure out all ingredients.

# Directions

1. Place sweet potatoes in a microwave-safe bowl and cover with vented plastic wrap. Microwave on high for 8 minutes, stirring once. (Move to Step 2 while waiting.) Remove and set aside.

2. Cut meat into 1" slices, then butterfly cut the slices by making cuts 3/4 through the meat. Open and flatten slightly each slice slightly.

3. Brush slices with 1 tbsp of soy sauce, and sprinkle lightly with pepper.

4. In a 12" skillet heat oil over medium-high heat.

5. Add garlic to skillet and cook just until golden. Remove garlic from skillet but keep skillet hot.

6. Add meat to hot skillet, and cook for 4-6 min, turning once. Remove from skillet, keeping warm, and keeping skillet hot.

7. Add sweet potatoes to hot skillet. Cook until potatoes begin to crisp, stirring occasionally.

8. Add green onions, cook and stir for 1 min.

9. Spoon hash onto individual plates, then top with meat and garlic. Keep skillet hot.

10. In the hot skillet, whisk together honey, water, and remaining soy sauce. Cook and stir until bubbly, then drizzle over meat.
